SOMETHING ABOUT THE DIVYA SOCIAL DEVLOPMENT CENTRE

Welcome to Divya Jyoti Social Development Center Since 2006

DIVYA JYOTI SOCIAL DEVLOPMENT CENTRE is a non profit making non government and secular grass root voluntary organization it is working in the villages of Dindori, Vidisha, Ujjain , Raisen, Bhopal & Sajapur District for the last Ten years in tribal areas for completely back ward and under poverty level people who are deprived most basic amenities of lively hood.

DJSDC is the developing, networking, capacity building, PCPNDT Programme, Environment, Asha Project, TB Projects, Gender Adoloents, Health, Aids, action oriented projects, RCH project Disabilities Women Awarness Ayusman Bharat Digital Mission , Mental Health, Mensuration Hygine and like-minded organizations for sustainable development.

OUR MISSION

An improvement in the quality of life of families living below the poverty line and other capacity buildings, skill development, income generation, primary education and promoting healthy including prevention measure TB, HIV & other incurable disease with special fuscous on women, children, tribal, disabled and Socio Economic any Backward people for birthing them into main stream of society.

OUR OBJECTIVES

To provide desk & field surveys, research & consultancy support. To creaste healthy environment for the deprived community and children improving hygience sanitation and water supply. To develop capacities of the local institutions and communities for sustainable development initiatives. To provide medical support to field level development organization. To provide technical and non technical support to field development organizations in planning implementation and monitoring of community based development projects. To demonstrate replicable strategies, process and action which can contribute to empower communities.

OUR VISION

To improve the health and socio economic status of rural people and strive for their overall development. To improve training for the strengthening of village, institution and also training other groups in the feld of sanitation, hygience and water supply. To implement innovative livelihood programmes with communities in different parts of M.P./UP strengthening the Women Empowerment and child care programme as well as Environment Awareness Programme. Networking with leading national and international organization in the development fields. Offering consultancy services to Govt. non Govt. & community based organization in the field of project Management.
